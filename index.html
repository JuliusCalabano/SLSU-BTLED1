A Brief Introduction to BTLED

The Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education (BTLED) program at Southern Luzon State University (SLSU) was established to develop skilled and competent educators in Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE). The program offers three specializations: Home Economics, which focuses on food, clothing, family management, and entrepreneurship; Industrial Arts, which develops technical skills in woodworking, electrical work, drafting, and basic mechanics; and Information and Communication Technology (ICT), which emphasizes computer systems, networking, programming, and digital tools. Through these specializations, the BTLED program prepares students with practical skills and teaching competencies for careers in education and community development.
